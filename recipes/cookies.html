Description

Chocolate. Chocolate, chocolate, chocolate. Hmmm!

Oh, sorry. Those chocolate chip cookies include almond, which is an allergen. If you're not allergic to almonds, they're easy, quick to cook and even quicker to eat.

Ingredients (10 cookies)

  • 100g powdered almonds
  • 200g flour
  • 1 pinch baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 50g sugar
  • 120g melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 200g chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients except chocolate chips and knead until you have a homogeneous ball.
  2. Put it in the fridge for 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the oven at 180 ‚Å∞C.
  4. Take out the cookie dough and add chocolate chips. Knead again.
  5. Make smaller balls, flatten them lightly and place them on an oven plate with baking paper.
  6. Bake for 13 minutes.

Feel free to adjust baking time if you like crustier cookies. Enjoy!

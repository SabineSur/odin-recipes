Roesti

  • Description

  • Ingredients

  • Steps

Description

Roesti is a sort of pancake made from grated potatoes. The recipe hails from the German-speaking part of Switzerland.

In fact, it's so typical that it gave rise to the phrase "Roesti frontier" to indicate the invisible line between the German-speaking part of the country and the French-speaking and Italian-speaking parts.

Roesti is a pretty good basis for a main meal. Some side dish suggestions include mushrooms and cream, or ham, chestnuts, etc.

Ingredients (serves 4)

  • 1 kg potatoes
  • A bit of butter or oil for cooking
  • You'll also need a large grate.

Steps

Don't forget to wash your hands carefully before handling foodstuff!

  1. Boil the potatoes until cooked throughout. This can be done with or without peeling them beforehand.
  2. Let them cool.
  3. Grate them over a large bowl.
  4. Warm up a pan on mid-heat.
  5. Add the butter or the oil in the pan.
  6. Shape the grated potatoes into balls. Their starch will keep them together.
  7. Place the balls in the pan and flatten them.
  8. Let them cook at least five minutes on each side.
  9. Serve as is or with the side dish of your choice.
